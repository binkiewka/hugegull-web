How to Use HugeGull

Basic Usage

  1. Paste a video URL (YouTube, Twitch, or direct stream)
  2. Select a Quick Preset
  3. Click "Generate Video"
  4. Wait for processing and download your compilation

Presets

  • YouTube: 16:9 aspect ratio, 45 seconds
  • TikTok/Shorts: 9:16 vertical, 30 seconds
  • Instagram: 1:1 square, 30 seconds
