How to Use HugeGull

Basic Usage

  1. Paste a video URL (YouTube, Twitch, or direct stream)
  2. Click "Generate Video"
  3. Wait for processing
  4. Download your clip compilation

Scene Detection

Enable "Scene Detection" for smarter cuts. Instead of random timestamps, HugeGull will find actual scene changes in the video for better transitions.

Presets

  • YouTube: 16:9 aspect ratio, 45 seconds
  • TikTok/Shorts: 9:16 vertical, 30 seconds
  • Instagram: 1:1 square, 30 seconds
  • Twitter: 16:9, 60 seconds

Tips

  • Skip intros/outros with Advanced Settings
  • Use GPU encoding for faster processing
  • Sort by "Most Dynamic First" for action highlights
